Rules:

  1. TKFI members should bring a minimum of 20 students to Karate Championships. If not, the masters can join with other TKFI members and make their students participate in tournaments.
  2. TKFI Members who conduct Karate Championships Karate functions and Belt Awarding functions should display TKFI Logo.
  3. TKFI should nominate at least one Referee in Karate tournaments.
  4. All TKFI members should participate in Referee training.
  5. Distribution of awards/prizes will be made category wise. Participation doesn't guarantee a prize. Decision on prize distribution is at the discretion of the referees.
  6. No remuneration is admissible to Masters during Tournaments, Belt Awarding functions and Referee Training.
  7. During world cup preparation period, each TKFI Member should nominate two students to the Group Committee so that the students will be given coaching.
  8. Members should give prior intimation to the TKFI Administration on date, venue and details of students participating in Karate Championships conducted.
  9. TKFI members should not form separate Association or members indulging in activities with the intention of dissolution of the TKFI or in any way acts detrimental to the interests of TKFI whatsoever, the TKFI reserves the right to cancel the membership and license.
  10. On the basis of legal proceedings, if any member is proved guilty of criminal activities, TKFI has the right to cancel the license.

Advantages of joining TKFI:

  1. The Student members of TFKI will be taken for World Level Karate Championships.
  2. All members will be trained by International Masters Kata/Kumite in Camps organized by TKFI.
  3. All members will be imparted to organize tournaments and will be assisted for smooth conduct of Karate tournaments.
  4. Members will be given all important inputs for referee training.
  5. Liaison with Government Agencies will be done by TKFI for recognition of the meritorious students in getting awards, medals for creating records.
  6. Meritorious student achievers from rural areas will be identified and encouraged and assisted to make achievements in the national/international level
  7. Girls from poor financial background will be trained in self defense.
  8. The members who win International Karate Championships will be assisted in getting Sports quota in Government jobs.
