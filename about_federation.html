Our motto is stronger people build stronger nation.

Who we are?

The Karate Federation of India (TKFI) is one of its kind organizations in the country comprising of Martial Arts educators of various styles. We are duly recognized by WUKF-World Union of Karate Do Federations and registered under Society Act. Being non-profitable organization, we constantly strive in upliftment of martial arts in our country by initiating awareness in Policymakers, Educators, Institutes, family members and students.

What we do?

We are constantly building and expanding our federation since 2014. We have enrolled Karate experts all over the country across various districts in India. We offer training programs to the karate experts in order to bring uniformity in the field. We have affiliated institutions where our karate experts get opportunity to coach the aspiring students.

Where are we?

In the year 2014, we started with 3 karate masters and were training students. Today we have expanded pan India to 20+ states and 100+ districts. We have trained 1,00,000+ students so far and have conducted 2000+ tournaments nationally and internationally.

Our vision

  1. Training karate educators with a uniform curriculum, so that they can further build a strong and healthy nation.
  2. Physical education of women with martial arts so that they can tackle unforeseen circumstances.
  3. Special focus on training the underprivileged so that learning martial arts is not their distant dream.
  4. Motivate students to take up karate as a career option by helping them participate in national and international tournaments.
  5. Spreading awareness amongst the people that martial arts is not limited to defence techniques, but a means to improve quality of life.
  6. Strive to bring basics of martial arts to be incorporated in standard sports curriculum in schools.
